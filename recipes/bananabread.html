The most Epic Banana Bread (for baking noobs)

Description

The banana bread. It is more cake than bread despite the name. Easy to prepare dough is ready in a flash by only mixing!

Ingredients

10 servings

  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 100 g melted butter
  • 1 dl farine sugar
  • half a cup of sugar
  • an egg
  • 3 cups of flour
  • 2 cups of oats
  • 1 cup of pecan nuts
  • 1 cup of raisins
  • 1 ts cinnamon
  • 1 ts cardemum
  • 1 ts baking soda
  • 1 ts salt

On the surface

  • 2 spoons of syrup
  • a banana
  • half a cup of pecan nuts

Steps

  1. Butter up a rectangle shaped oven bowl.
  2. Squish the bananas with a fork.
  3. Mix up cooled melted butter, sugars, egg and mashed bananas.
  4. Mix up the dry ingredients and add into the butter.
  5. Pour dough into the oven bowl. Drip suryp on top and garnish with a split banana and pecan nuts.
  6. Roast the banana bread for 50 minutes on the mid level of a 175 degree oven. If the bread starts to darken beyond your taste, cover it up with baking paper.
  7. Let the bread cool before removing it from the bowl.
  8. Serve this epic banana bread with peanut butter for instance!
