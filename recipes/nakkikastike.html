Weiner sauce

Please note that you can also make this dish using vegetarian weiners.

Description

Using this recipe, the familiar everyday favorite meal is done and ready to eat in less than 30 minutes. Serve with mashed or boiled potatoes

Steps

  • 1 onion
  • 300 g weiners
  • 1 ts butter
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 4 cups of water
  • 2 ts ketchup
  • 1/2 ts mustarc
  • approx. 1/2 whipped cream
  • pinch of black pepper

Steps

  1. Chop up onion and dice the weiners.
  2. Heat up the butter and onions for a couple of minutes. Add weiners. Let turn brown and sprinkle flour to the surface. Mix well.
  3. Pour in water. Mix and boil with mild heat for 3-5 minutes
  4. Season with ketchup, mustard, cream and black pepper. Garnish with parsley (optional). Serve with mashed potateos, green salad and rye bread.
