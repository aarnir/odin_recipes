Cherry Tomato Soup

Description

My colleague gave me a bunch of cherry tomatoes. I was wondering if I'm able to use them before they expire. So I had an idea to make a delicious tomato soup.

I wanted a simple yet tasty recipe. I also wanted to utilize most of the ingredients I found in my fridge. So this is what I came up with:

Ingredients

  • 3 boxes of cherry tomatoes
  • An onion
  • 2 pieces of garlic
  • Philadelphia cheese
  • Grated cheese

Steps

  1. Chop onion and garlic
  2. Warm them in a kettle (don't roast!)
  3. Throw tomatoes into kettle
  4. Let simmer for 5 minutes
  5. Use an appliance to make it into fine soup
  6. Add Philadelphia cheese and Grated cheese
  7. Let simmer for 15 minutes
  8. Serve!
